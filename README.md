### 👋 Hi, nice to see you, I’m Shaun
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 👀 I’m a Software Engineer at Volvo Cars with a background that started in 11th grade, when I began building with C, Arduino, and PCB design. That early hands-on path led me to design a cute robot dog and sparked a long-term interest in learning new technologies and building things from scratch.

### 🚀 Startup Experience
During my undergrad, I co-founded my first startup, which was selected as a Top 100 Taiwan Startup in 2021 and showcased our product at CES 2021. During my Master's degree, I also worked at a startup in the USA, which gave me broad hands-on experience in fast-paced, high-ownership environments.

### 🛠️ Hardware / Embedded
I started with low-level programming and embedded hardware, then expanded into industrial automotive ECU development. Along the way, I built experience with MISRA C, ISO 26262, and embedded workflows that emphasize safety, reliability, and engineering discipline.

### 👨‍💻 Full-Stack Software
I worked on full-stack software during my startup journey, where I built hands-on experience in fast-moving environments and learned how to ship quickly. At Volvo Cars, I deepened that experience in real-world production systems, contributing to large-scale products used by millions of users while expanding my skills in Java, Spring Boot, REST APIs, databases, CI/CD, agile development at scale, and cross-functional collaboration.

🎓 I hold a Master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Rice University, specializing in Computer Engineering and Software Engineering. 🦉
